## About Me

I love asking questions at the intersection of environment, data, and people—and then chasing the answers with purpose.

My work focuses on turning complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. Through data analysis and visualization, I uncover patterns that drive understanding and real-world impact. I’m particularly passionate about air quality, human health, and using data to promote equity in STEM and beyond.

Curiosity, intention, and responsibility guide everything I do—whether I’m building analyses, telling stories with data, or creating space for others through mentorship and representation.

- 🔬 Research & Analysis: experimental data → clear, defensible conclusions
- 📊 Skills: R (tidyverse, ggplot2), Excel (pivot tables, dashboards)
- 🧠 Strengths: data wrangling, visualization, interpretation, science communication
- 🌱 Community: supporting Latino researchers through programming, mentoring, and access
- 🌐 Bilingual: English / Spanish
